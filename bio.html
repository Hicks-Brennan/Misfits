Photographer

Acacia Hathaway

I have always had a passion for photography but finally pursued it professionally five years ago. My work is unique. I enjoy taking photos and turning them into works of art. Ever wanted super powers? I use professional software to digitally create your dream and make it a reality. I have created so many works of art including gig flyers, logos, book covers, as well as photographed weddings, babies, senior photos, sports teams, festivals, even a girl and her cat. I am excited to be a part of this team and expand my passion for art. Misfits is as quirky and awe inspiring as Peculiar Pixels. We are a match made in artistry heaven.
